# Code of Conduct

This project follows the
[Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

- Be respectful. Disagreements about technical direction are fine —
  attacks on people are not.
- Assume good faith. Most miscommunication on the internet is people
  reading tone into text that isn't there.
- Focus the discussion on the code, the design, the user. Not on the
  contributor.
- No harassment, slurs, or sexualized content in any project space
  (issues, PRs, discussions).

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation:

- Email **jungho@manycoresoft.co.kr**.
- Include screenshots / links / quotes where possible.
- Reports are confidential. We will not retaliate against good-faith reporters.

The maintainer responds within 3 business days. Outcomes can range from
a private note, to a public clarification, to removal from the project's
community spaces. We do not run tribunals — we make the call we think
keeps the project healthy.
